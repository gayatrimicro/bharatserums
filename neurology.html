Thymogam
 
Brand Description Thymogam is a sterile, transparent to slightly opalescent aqueous colorless to pale yellow solution containing Antithymocyte globulin. Thymogam is obtained by processing hyper-immune serum of horses immunized with human thymocytes.
Category IMMUNOGLOBULINS & ANTI-SERA
Indications Renal tranplant: Management of Allograft rejection and as an adjunct along with the conventional therapy to delay the onset of 1st rejection episode.
Aplastic Anemia: treatment of moderate to severe aplastic anaemia in patients not suitable for bone marrow transplant
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation Each vial (5ml) containing 100mg / 250mg of Antithymocyte Globulin (equine)
Storage 2- 8°C

AMPHONEX
 
Brand Description AMPHONEX (Liposomal Amphotericin B Injection I.P.) is a liposomal formulation containing Amphotericin B intercalated into the lipid bilayer. It is a lyophilized sterile product for intravenous infusion.
Category ANTI-FUNGALS
Indications To treat invasive fungal infection in patients who are refractory to or intolerant of conventional amphotericin B therapy.

Dysport
 
Brand Description Dysport Powder for Injection contains 500 IPSEN UNITS per vial of Clostridium botulinum type A toxin-haemagglutinin complex, 125 microgram human serum albumin and 2.5 mg lactose in a sterile, lyophilised form without a preservative. Clostridium botulinum type A toxin-haemagglutinin complex has a molecular weight of about 900,000D and is a complex of proteins
Category Neuromuscular blocker
Indications Adult Arm Spasticity, Cervical Dystonia, cerebral palsy, blepherospasm, hemifacial spasm
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation Pack of 1 or 2 vials
Storage 2- 8°C

KEMODOXA
 
Brand Description KEMODOXA (Doxorubicin Hydrochloride Liposome Injection) is a sterile translucent red liquid containing Doxorubicin Hydrochloride encapsulated in liposomes. Doxorubicin Hydrochloride is a Cytotoxic anthracycline antibiotic obtained from Streptomyces peucetius var. caesius and is used in the treatment of cancers
Category CYTOTOXIC
Indications Metastatic Breast Cancer:
As a monotherapy in women where there is an increased Cardiac Risk.
In women for whom an anthracycline would be considered.
In women who have failed a Taxane containing regimen.
Metastatic Carcinoma of the ovary
In patients with diseases refractory to both Paclitaxel and Platinum based chemotherapy.
AIDS- related Kaposi's sarcoma.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation 20mg / 10ml; 50mg / 25ml
Storage 2- 8°C
