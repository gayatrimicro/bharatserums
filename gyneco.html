Rhoclone
 
Brand Description Rhoclone is a Monoclonal Human Anti-rhesus Immunoglobulin (lgG1) containing antibodies to Rho-D. It is derived from stable hybridoma cell line and purified using chromatography. It is available as both lyophilized and liquid formulation.
Category Immunoglobulins
Indications Prevents Rho-D negative women forming antibodies to foetal rhesus-positive red blood cells that may pass into the maternal blood during childbirth, abortion or certain other sensitising events.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation 100 mcg / 150 mcg / 300 mcg (lyophilized / liquid)
Storage 2- 8°C

Primigyn
 
Brand Description Primigyn (Dinoprostone) Gel 0.5 mg is a naturally-occurring Prostaglandin E2 (PGE2). In pregnancy, PGE2 is secreted continuously by the fetal membranes and placenta and plays an important role in the final events leading to the initiation of labor. It is known that PGE2 stimulates the production of PGF2 which in turn sensitizes the myometrium to endogenous or exogenously administrated oxytocin, this stimulation of the myometrium of the gravid uterus causes it to contract in a manner similar to contractions seen in the term uterus during labor.
Category Prostaglandin E2
Indications For ripening and dilatation of an unfavorable cervix for labour induction.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation 0.5 mg Dinoprostone BP per 3.0 g gel in a syringe with a catheter for endocervical application.
Storage 2- 8°C

Hucog HP
 
Brand Description Hucog HP is brand of Human Chorionic Gonadotrophin hormone (Highly Purified). It is a time tested quality product. No reconstitution required with ease of administration. It is the only liquid stable HCG in India. It is available as lyophilized formulation in few markets.
Category GONADOTROPINS
Indications Anovulatory infertility : In the female, HUCOG HP is used in the treatment of anovulatory infertility. where its administration would form part of recognized treatment regimen involving prior stimulation of follicular maturation and endometrial proliferation e.q. with Menotropin Injection (HUMOG HP). Hypogonadotrophin hypogonadism and cryptorchidism : In the male, HUCOG HP stimulates the interstitial cells of the testes and consequently the secretion of androgens and the development of secondary sexual characteristics. With concomitant Menotropin Injection therapy, HUCOG HP stimulates the induction and maintenance of spermatogenesis.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation 2000/5000/10000 IU (Liquid and Lyophilised)
Storage 2- 8°C
