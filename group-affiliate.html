Group / Affiliate Companies

  • Aksigen

  • Kasiak Research Pvt. Ltd.

  • Next Line Diagnostics

Advy Chemical Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in 1991 and has been servicing the global In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD) industry ever since. Over the years, Advy Chemical has developed into a globally recognized provider of high quality reagents and components specifically tailored to suit the needs of its customers.

Today, the company exports its immunodiagnostic reagent products to customers spread across the Americas, Europe, China and Japan. Advy has also entered the domestic market with the manufacture of lateral flow assays, in its state-of-the-art production facility.

For more information please visit - www.advychemical.com

AKSIGEN is a research driven pharmaceutical company dedicated to putting patients first. AKSIGEN discovers, develops, manufactures and markets specialized healthcare products to meet the needs of the quality conscious market.

For more information please visit - www.aksigen.com

Kasiak Research Private Limited is a research and development company with its focus in the field of Regenerative Medicine. Kasiak aims to develop products that redefine Regenerative Medicine through innovation, team work and integrity. This is exemplified by Kasiak's high quality product pipeline which helps provide Regenerative Cellutions to address unmet medical needs.

For more information please visit - www.kasiakresearch.com

Nextline Diagnostics' mission is to offer solutions to the medical community and patients that go beyond the diagnosis. We focus on bridging the gap between diagnosis and treatment, offering predictive diagnosis and preventive therapeutics. We aim to provide a complete solution to the doctor and patient and make the diagnosis a more accurate process. The Company's core competency is the knowledge and experience of its scientists, technicians and associates in the field of diagnostics and therapeutics. The Company's legal form is that of an LLP in India.

For more information please visit - www.nextlinediagnostics.com
