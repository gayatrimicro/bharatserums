Manufacturing - Locations

Injectable Plant

Ambernath, Maharashtra, India
Quick Info
  • The Company's injectables manufacturing plant is located in Ambernath, approximately 60 kilometers from Mumbai.
  • It houses 4 dedicated injectable lines targeting India and emerging markets
  • This plant has capabilities for lyophilised and liquid injectables, and has been built as per UK MHRA guidelines
  • Spanning 65,000 square feet, BSV's injectable plant has a capacity to produce 15 million vials per year.

Biotech API & Injectable Plant

Ambernath, Maharashtra, India
Quick Info
  • BSV has a built a state of the art biotech plant, which is also located in Ambernath, approximately 60 kilometers from Mumbai
  • This plant was commissioned in September 2011
  • Special focus is biotech and biological products. The plant has capabilities to manufacture commercial scale mammalian cell cultured derived recombinant proteins and fill and finished capabilities for liquid and lyophilised injectable products.

Horse Farm

Maharashtra, India
Quick Info
  • The Company has a horse farm, situated in the outskirts of Mumbai.
  • The horse farm has a total strength of approximately 400 horses
  • BSV is in the process of establishing new horse farms with partners, which will increase the total capacity multifold
  • The horse farm has facilities for the immunization of animals, plasmapheresis, plasma separation and on-site testing under the supervision of qualified veterinary doctors
  • The horse farms has the requisite certification from the CPCSEA and local drug authorities
