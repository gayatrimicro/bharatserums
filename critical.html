U-TRYP
 
Brand Description Ulinastatin is a serine protease inhibitor that reduces the pro-inflammatory response as a result of sepsis, acute pancreatitis, trauma or surgery. Ulinastatin for Injection is available as clear colourless liquid and as lyoplilized formulation.
Category Serine protease inhibitor
Indications 1. Severe Sepsis (due to infection, trauma, burns or surgery)
2. Mild and severe acute pancreatitis.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation 50,000 I.U. / 1,00,000 I.U.
Storage 2- 8°C

Gamma IV
 
Brand Description Gamma IV is a brand of Normal Human immunoglobulin 5 % for Intravenouse use . It is a sterile solution containing 4.5 %-5.5 % of human protein in 9-11 % maltose to achieve isotonicity. It contains no preservatives . Each ml contains approximately 50 mg of protein. Also present are traces of lgA and lgM. The distribution of lgG subclasses is similar to that found in normal human serum.
Category Immunoglobulins
Indications 1. Primary Immunodeficiency (PID).
2. Kawasaki syndrome.
3. Idiopathic Thrombocytopenic Purpura (ITP).
4. Bone Marrow Transplantation.
5. Chronic B-cell Lymphocytic Leukemia.
6. Pediatric HIV-1 Infection.
7. Neonatal Sepsis.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation 0.5 gm / 10 ml , 2.5 gm/ 50 ml , 5 gm /100 ml
Storage 2- 8°C

THROMBOFLUX
 
Brand Description THROMBOFLUX is a freeze-dried white powder containing Streptokinase. Strepto-kinase is a highly purified protein derived from streptococci species. Streptokinase has the property of combining with human plasminogen to form plasminogen activator. One International unit is the quantity of Streptokinase that will lyse the standard blood clot completely in 10 minutes.
Category THROMBOLYTICS
Indications 1. Acute evolving transmural myocardial infarction.
2. Acute deep vein thrombosis.
3. Acute Pulmonary embolism.
4. Acute arterial thrombosis or embolism.
5. Occlusion of arterio-venous cannulae.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation Vial packs of 2,50,000 I.U., 7,50,000 I.U. and 15,00,000 I.U.
Storage 2- 8°C

AMPHOTRET
 
Brand Description AMPHOTRET (Amphotericin B For Injection) is a freeze dried preparation of Amphotericin B for intravenous injection. AMPHOTRET is a yellow coloured cake which is to be reconstituted with Sterile Water for Injection before administration. Amphotericin B is a polyene macrolide antifungal antibiotic obtained from a strain of Streptomyces nodosus.
Category ANTI-INFECTIVE
Indications Visceral leishmaniasis (Kala Azar)
Patients with progressive, potentially life-threatening fungal infections.
Management of Invasive fungal infections
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation 50mg / vial
Storage 2- 8°C

AMPHOMUL
 
AMPHOMUL AMPHOMUL (Amphotericin B Emulsion) is a sterile, yellow coloured liquid for intravenous infusion containing Amphotericin B suspended in an oil-in-water emulsion available in 50 mg/10 ml preparation. Amphomul is a proprietory product of BSV and has various formulation patents (U.S., Europe, South Africa, Japan, Australia etc.) The major goal of developing ABLE has been to attain a novel AmB preparation with lower toxicity and with at least similar efficacy compared to the parent compound, amphotericin B deoxycholate, at an economic price so that its benefits can be extended to the masses.
Category ANTI-FUNGAL
Indications Visceral leishmaniasis (Kala Azar) Febrile neutropenia in Cancer Patients.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation 50 mg /10 ml
Storage Store below 25°C

U-FRAG
 
Brand Description U-FRAG is a freeze-dried white powder containing an enzyme Urokinase. It is isolated from fresh human urine. It activates plasminogen with the subsequent hydrolysis of fibrin and the dissolution of thrombin. Urokinase is a mixture of low (33,000) and high (54,000) molecular weight forms.
Category Thrombolytics
Indications Cerebral thrombosis, Cerebral infarction. Acute Peripheral vascular thrombo-embolism. Acute myocardial infarction. Acute Pulmonary embolism. Clearance of occluded catheters and shunts.
Dosage & Administration Please refer pack insert for further details
Presentation Vial pack of 5000 I.U. and 500000 I.U.
Storage 2- 8°C
