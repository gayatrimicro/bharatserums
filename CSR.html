Corporate Social Responsibility

  • The Society of the Franciscan – Home for Senior Citizens Old Goa

    Bharat Serums and Vaccines Limited (BSVL) has contributed through Trust for Construction of ‘Home for the Senior Citizens’ for providing necessary support, services, facilities, etc. to the Senior Citizens.

  • Kamaldham Vrudhashram and Kalyankari Sanstha

    BSVL has contributed through Trust for installation of Solar Energy lights at Vrudhashram for saving in electricity charges.

  • Shrimad Rajchandra Sarvamangal Trust (Mumbai Marathon - 2017)

    BSVL has contributed through Trust for Treatment for Cancer Patients.

  • The Rotary Club of Bombay – Cotton Green

    BSVL has contributed through Trust to provide paediatric medical check-up at the clinic for the underprivileged residents in the cotton Green and nearby area in Mumbai.

  • Think Peace Organization

    BSVL has contributed through Trust towards “Arogyam” program which is aimed for creating a sustainable healthcare model to the inhabitant tribes in Araku valley, Andhra Pradesh.

  • Children Of The World (India) Trust

    BSVL has contributed through trust for Child Care activities for orphans at Nerul, Navi Mumbai.

  • ML Dhawale Trust

    BSVL has contributed to Trust which runs rural Homoeopathic Hospital in Palghar for needy patients.

  • Sau Mathurabai Bhausaheb Thorat Sevabhavi Trust, Sangamner

    BSVL has contributed to the trust which runs charitable hospital in northern parts of Maharashtra for tribal masses residing in Igatpuri, along-with other Talukas of the vicinity.

  • Americares India Foundation

    BSVL has contributed for procurement and distribution of the medicines to the poor, un-affording people, research and study project to identify, recognize and share best practices towards the cause of 'Women's Health Empowerment.

  • Prem Seva Mahila Mandal

    BSVL has contributed towards day Meals of 500 such children for the period of 1 year and has also provided school books, raincoats, etc. to them. These children are from the hamlets/padas on the outskirts of the city of Kalyan.

  • The Rotary Club of Bombay – Colaba

    BSVL has contributed through Trust in Project " Bhavishyaan" for enhancement of Conversational English, I.T., & Life Skills of the students of Colaba Municipal School.

  • Shramik Naari Sangh

    Women Empowerment - BSVL has contributed for purchase of ‘Khakra making Machines’ to the tribal women and empowering them to earn livelihoods.

  • The Rotary Club of Bombay – YMCA

    BSVL has contributed towards vocational training conducted by YMCA to the underprivileged women at Methodist Church, Mumbai Central, Mumbai.
