Manufacturing - Quality

"Quality is not an act. It is a habit." – Aristotle

These words are the foundation upon which BSV's quality cell is built. In fact, they are deeply rooted in the Company's culture as a whole. We at BSV have a firm commitment of all our personnel, involved in various activities of the company, to assure the quality of our products and deliver products that conform to fitness of purpose at all levels i.e. a firm commitment to market products with highest "'QUALITY", conforming to the laid down specifications and adherence to cGMP regulations at all functional areas.

QUALITY CONTROL

"QUALITY CONTROL" is the core functional area of our quality activity. The Quality Control system encompasses controls and checks at every stage of the manufacturing process beginning with raw materials and progressing till the finished product is dispatched for sale.

BSV's Quality Control Laboratory is managed through the following four sections and is well equipped with sophisticated instruments & approved technical personnel, thus ensuring a highly effective and efficient process.

  • Chemical Testing Laboratory.
  • Instrument Room.
  • Microbiological Laboratory.
  • Animal testing Laboratory.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

BSV's Quality Assurance Department, staffed by competent technical personnel and is broadly classified into the following functional departments.

  • Quality Management System/ Documentation cell
  • Validation / Calibration
  • In process Quality Assurance (IPQA)
  • Training

The above departments assure that quality is built into each and every operations that is carried out in BSV's manufacturing facilities

Quality Policy - Commitment To Care For Life

Our strong belief in quality is not restricted by just conformance to specification but to develop a team of personnel that believes in quality as a way of life. It is our constant endeavour to build quality at every stage in the manufacture of our products by means of using:

  • Best Available Resources.
  • Good Product Design.
  • Well-Equipped Production Departments.
  • Well-Trained Qualified Personnel.
  • An effective Quality Assurance System.
